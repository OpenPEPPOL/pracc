
= Business process and use cases

The {link-bpmn}[BPMN] diagram shows the choreography of the business process implemented by the {name-profile}. The choreography of business collaborations defines the sequence of interactions when the profile is run within its context.

image::bpmnQualificationRejection.svg[align="center", width=800]

[cols="2,10", options="header"]
.Business process
|===
| Category | Description
| Description | The contracting body sends the rejection of the qualification to the economic operator that submitted a qualification which was not accepted by the contracting authority.

| Pre-conditions | Economic operators applied for participating in the tendering process and submitted their qualifications.
| Post-conditions | Those economic operators who received a qualification rejection will not be invited to tender in the next step of the tendering process.
|===

[cols="3,2,7", options="header"]
.Role description
|===
| Activity | Role involved | Description
| Send qualification rejection | Contracting body | The contracting body sends an electronic qualification rejection to those economic operators that will be excluded from the further tendering process due to qualification mismatch.
| Receive qualification rejection | Economic operator | The economic operator receives the qualification rejection.
|===
